## What Are MIL Spec Components?

**MIL spec** (Military Specification) components are electronic parts that conform to rigorous U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) standards. These specifications ensure that components can endure extreme environmental, electrical, and mechanical stresses.

### Common MIL Standards Followed by Masline
- **MIL-STD-883:** Microelectronic test methods and qualification.  
- **MIL-PRF-19500:** General performance requirements for semiconductor devices.  
- **MIL-STD-202:** Testing procedures for electronic and electrical components.  
- **MIL-DTL-38999:** Circular connectors for high-reliability applications.  
- **MIL-STD-810:** Environmental testing for endurance and ruggedness.  

Masline’s adherence to these standards ensures consistency and compatibility across military, aerospace, and industrial systems.
